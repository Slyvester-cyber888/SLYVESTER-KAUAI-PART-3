Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about franchise options or catering services? Find quick answers below.

What are the capital requirements for a Kauai Franchise?

+

A standard Kauai store requires an average capital investment of R1.2M to R1.8M, depending on the format (retail mall, standalone, or health club). Applicants must have a minimum of 50% unencumbered cash.

How much advance notice is required for corporate catering?

+

We require at least 24 hours advance notice for standard catering platter orders. For larger groups or events exceeding 100 people, we recommend contacting us 3 to 5 business days in advance to ensure slot availability.

Can you customize food platters for specific dietary needs?

+

Yes, absolutely! We offer extensive options for vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free, and ketogenic diets. Simply specify your dietary needs in the message field of your enquiry, and our chefs will customize the menu accordingly.

Do you offer sponsorships for sports events?

+

Kauai is highly passionate about wellness and fitness! We review event sponsorship proposals on a monthly basis. Please submit your event size, audience profile, and partnership opportunities through our enquiry form under "Sponsorship/Events" category.
