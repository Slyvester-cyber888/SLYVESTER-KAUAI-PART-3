## 1. Organisation Overview

### Name
**Kauai**

### Brief History
Kauai is a South African health food restaurant chain founded in 1995 in Cape Town. The brand was built on the principle that healthy food should be accessible, delicious, and convenient — a positioning that set it apart from conventional fast-food competitors at the time. Beginning with a focus on freshly prepared wraps, salads, smoothies, and juices, Kauai quickly gained a loyal following among health-conscious consumers and fitness communities across the country.

Over the following three decades, Kauai expanded from a small Cape Town outlet into a national franchise with over 170 locations across South Africa, including standalone stores, gym-based kiosks, and shopping centre outlets. The brand is particularly prominent in fitness and wellness environments, with a strong presence inside Virgin Active, Planet Fitness, and similar health club facilities — making it highly relevant to active lifestyle audiences.

### Mission and Vision
- **Mission:** To nourish South Africans with real, wholesome food that fuels an active and balanced lifestyle, made fresh to order every day.
- **Vision:** To be the most loved healthy fast-food brand in South Africa, recognised for quality ingredients, bold flavours, and a commitment to wellness.

### Target Audience
Kauai's primary audience comprises health-conscious adults aged 18 to 45, including gym-goers, athletes, professionals seeking nutritious lunch options, and families interested in clean eating. The brand also targets schools and corporates as secondary audiences for catering and bulk meal solutions.

## 2. Website Goals and Objectives
The proposed website will function as Kauai's primary digital touchpoint, designed to attract new customers, communicate brand values, display the menu, and facilitate enquiries. The specific goals are as follows:

- **Increase brand awareness:** Present Kauai's story, values, and product range to new and existing customers through an engaging, informative online presence.
- **Showcase the menu:** Display Kauai's full product range including meals, wraps, smoothies, and seasonal specials with appealing imagery and nutritional context.
- **Drive foot traffic:** Use store location pages and a map to direct customers to their nearest Kauai outlet.
- **Generate franchise and catering enquiries:** Provide a structured enquiry form for potential franchisees and corporate catering clients.
- **Support brand loyalty:** Communicate promotions, health tips, and rewards programme information to returning visitors.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Monthly unique visitors (target: 8,000+ within 3 months of launch)
- Menu page view rate (target: 60%+ of sessions include a menu page visit)
- Enquiry form submissions (target: 30+ per month)
- Store locator interactions (tracked via click rate on location links)
- Bounce rate (target: below 50% on homepage)

## 3. Current Website Analysis

### Strengths
- The current Kauai website (kauai.co.za) presents a visually appealing homepage with strong food photography and a clear brand identity built around green tones and wellness messaging.
- The site includes a functional store locator, an online ordering integration, and a nutritional information section.

### Weaknesses and Areas for Improvement
- The menu navigation is inconsistent across device sizes, making it difficult for mobile users to browse products efficiently.
- The franchise and corporate enquiry pathways are not clearly signposted, resulting in missed leads.
- The About Us section lacks depth — brand history and values are underrepresented relative to the strength of the Kauai story.
- Certain pages have slow load times due to large, uncompressed image files.

The proposed website will address these gaps with a streamlined, mobile-first design, a richer brand narrative, and a clearly structured enquiry page tailored to multiple user types.

## 4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality

### Essential Pages
- **Homepage (`index.html`):** Full-width hero section with a brand statement and call to action, featured menu highlights, a wellness philosophy section, and a store locator prompt.
- **About Us (`about.html`):** Brand origin story, mission and vision, health philosophy, and key milestones — told with imagery and engaging copy.
- **Menu / Products (`products.html`):** Category-organised menu listings (wraps, salads, smoothies, juices, breakfast, sides) with item names, descriptions, and imagery.
- **Enquiry (`enquiry.html`):** Structured form accommodating franchise enquiries, corporate catering requests, and general customer questions, with appropriate field sets per enquiry type.
- **Contact (`contact.html`):** Store contact details, embedded map showing multiple Kauai locations (Cape Town, Johannesburg, Gqeberha), and a general contact form.

### Additional Functionality
- Responsive navigation with active state indicators
- Image assets sourced and optimised from Creative Commons and official brand resources
- Internal anchor links on the menu page for quick category navigation
- Semantic HTML throughout with full comment documentation

## 5. Design and User Experience

### Design Aesthetic
Kauai's design language is rooted in freshness, vitality, and natural wellness. The proposed website will reflect this through the use of organic green tones, clean white backgrounds, and vibrant food photography. The overall atmosphere should feel welcoming, energetic, and aligned with a healthy lifestyle — approachable without being clinical.

- **Colour Scheme:**
  - Primary — Fresh Green (`#4CAF50` / `#2E7D32`)
  - Secondary — Warm White (`#FAFAFA`)
  - Accent — Burnt Orange (`#E65100`)
  - Supporting — Light Stone (`#F5F0E8`)
- **Typography:**
  - Headings — Rounded or humanist sans-serif (Nunito or Poppins)
  - Body — Clean and readable sans-serif (Lato or Open Sans)
  - Hierarchy expressed through weight and scale variation.
- **Layout and Design:** Card-based layout for menu items, generous padding for a premium feel, and full-width imagery sections between content blocks. Navigation is sticky and simple, with no more than five top-level items.

### User Experience Considerations
- **Navigation:** Clear and flat — each page reachable within one click from the homepage.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast text on all backgrounds, meaningful alt text for all images, and keyboard-navigable structure.
- **Mobile responsiveness:** Designed mobile-first, with touch-friendly tap targets and stacked layouts on smaller screens.
- **Calls to action:** Present on every page — guiding users toward the menu, a store visit, or an enquiry submission.

### Wireframe Overview
Wireframes have been conceptualised for all five pages. The homepage features a large hero section, a three-column feature row, and a promotional strip leading into the footer. The menu page uses a filterable grid of item cards. The contact page uses a two-column layout with location cards on the left and a form on the right.
